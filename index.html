Common Questions

When should I book my first prenatal visit?

Ideally, you should schedule your first visit as soon as you confirm your pregnancy (usually around 6-8 weeks) to ensure a healthy start.

How often should I get a Pap smear?

Most women should have a Pap smear every 3 years starting at age 21, or as recommended based on their individual health history.

Can PCOS be managed without heavy medication?

Yes, many cases are successfully managed through specialized diets, exercise, and targeted hormonal support tailored to the patient.

Is a gynecological exam painful?

While you may feel brief pressure, an exam should not be painful. Our clinic prioritizes patient comfort and gentle techniques.
