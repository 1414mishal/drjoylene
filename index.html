About the Doctor

A dedicated voice in women's healthcare.

Dr. Joylene Diana D'Almeida is an experienced Obstetrician and Gynecologist with deep expertise in comprehensive women's healthcare. With a strong academic background and years of clinical experience, she combines evidence-based medicine with compassionate, personalized care for every patient.

She is a Professor of Obstetrics & Gynecology at Father Muller Medical College Hospital, an Active Member of MOGS, FOGSI, PCOS Society of India, WALS, AMAS and CHD, an FOGSI-approved Critical Care Obstetrics trainer, and Co-founder of Moms of Mangalore — a 37,000-strong community focused on women's health and empowerment.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment with Dr. Joylene? expand_more

For Pulse Polyclinic, the appointment line is +91 96635 57319 (call or WhatsApp). The clinic reception is +91 98804 87755. For The Olive Clinics, call +91 63625 32742. Walk-in consultations are subject to availability.

What are the consultation hours at Pulse Polyclinic? expand_more

Weekdays from 1:30 PM to 2:30 PM and 5:00 PM to 7:00 PM at Pulse Polyclinic, K.S. Rao Road, Mangaluru.

Does Dr. Joylene perform laparoscopic surgery? expand_more

Yes. Dr. Joylene holds a Fellowship in Minimal Access Surgery (FMAS) from World Laparoscopy Hospital and performs laparoscopic hysterectomy, ovarian cyst removal, fibroid management, endometriosis surgery, hysteroscopy and TVT-O sling procedures, among others.

Does she handle high-risk pregnancies? expand_more

Yes — high-risk pregnancy management is one of her core areas of expertise, alongside normal and assisted vaginal deliveries and cesarean sections (elective and emergency).

Does she treat PCOS, irregular cycles and menopause concerns? expand_more

Absolutely. Menstrual disorders (PCOS, irregular cycles, heavy bleeding), menopause management and hormonal health are all part of her practice — with a strong focus on lifestyle planning and preventive care.

Are adolescent and preventive consultations available? expand_more

Yes. Adolescent gynecology, preventive women's health screening, cervical cancer screening, HPV vaccination and contraception counseling (IUCD, implants, pills) are routinely offered.

Where can I read patient reviews? expand_more

You can read verified reviews on Google. We also feature several real patient testimonials on this page.
